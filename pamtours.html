PamTours

Web Design

Home / portfolio

Designing a website for Pam Tours - a tourist agency with daily guaranteed departures from the major tourist centres in the Opatija region. After the initial interview with the client, who already had a design in mind the next step was to design the predetermined visual design and iterate where needed.

Due to the necessity of the website to be online as soon as possible the wireframes were done on paper. The content was translated into 3 languages and implemented into the website.
