Laura's story

Ambitious seeker of knowledge thriving on learning new things, a broad thinker with a sharp mind with a background in business and languages...uh and let’s do not forget a massive coffee lover with a hell of a sense of humour.

Expressing my creativity is something I have pursued my whole life, both professionally and individually. As far as I can remember as a child I used to express it through drawing, sketching, painting, crafting and even dancing and as an adult - designing appealing visual content.

I come from a business professional background where I combined my business degree with linguistics and education. The latter sharpened my skills as a user experience designer, a position I have been working for the last couple of years.

Now my foremost passion is USER EXPERIENCE and everything associated with it. I love what I do and truly take pride in providing high-quality design solutions by combining research, technology and beautiful design.

hmm...what else?

Have a Master’s Degree in User Experience Design 🤓 completed at Kingston University, Faculty of Science, Engineering and Computing.

When I am not working I love exploring different art styles or exploring cities and capturing the moments...another interest - Photography;

Then ... morning yoga, scuba diving in Mošćenička (read: Moschenichka) Draga, Croatia or just having a nice cuppa (..two,three...) in Ljubljana.

.. and last but not least - watching “everything-that-has-to-do-with-supernatural” movies/tv-shows. Did I mention dancing?
