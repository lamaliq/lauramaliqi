Pension Buba

WEBDESIGN, BRANDING & TRANSLATING

In this project I acted as a researcher, designer and a translator.

'Pension Buba' is a family business owned by a lovely couple, who wanted a simple webpage that would present the main features they have to offer and what people can do while they are visiting. After the initial interview with the client, next step was to identify the needs within the business, and determin the best solutions to meet the business's needs.

There were two main functions required for this site: to be created in a way that can be edited by the owners themselves and to keep the website clean and simple. Therefore the website was build with the website builder WIX.

Visit Pension Buba to see the live site.
